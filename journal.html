Documentation of the process The Goal My goal was the design a website that would be typical of a Dermatologist office or a MediSpa. Design Process First I made a rough edit of what I thought I wanted to make based on the other businesses websites linked to me from the owners of the business. I made this in Affinity. I opened my code editor and saved my HTML and CSS files and got to work. I first coded out the layout of the page before adding any images or text. Like sections, header and footer links, etc Then to the CSS I did the same thing with laying out what I thought I might need to add properties too Then it was a matter of googling and remembering how to do the stuff I wanted
